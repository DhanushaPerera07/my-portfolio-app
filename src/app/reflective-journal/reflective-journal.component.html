Reflective Journal

This semester, I participated in the `Preparation for the Professional World - IT4070` module lectures. Since the lectures are conducted by scheduled Microsoft Teams meetings, all the co-students and lecturers were joined for the online discussion.

Since I need to refresh on topics like how to write a business letter, emails, and memos, I was interested in participating in the lectures. However, I could not join the lectures as they happened since I work in a company. Although I managed to refer to the recorded lecture materials continuously.

One of the essential things I learned from these sessions is how to format business letters and write a letter more efficiently. Before joining these lessons, I had done formatting and punctuation issues when writing emails. Since I had not written memos, I had little experience with when to use memos or how to write memos. When writing emails, I have not considered using a digital signature. I thought professional digital signatures were not something for private email users. Another relevant thing I learned from the sessions is the consideration of the tone when writing and how I express myself. During the class, we discussed these issues and corrected them by referring to examples.

Regarding Interview Skills, leadership, and public speaking skills sessions, I have to get hands-on experiences in the future. After I referred to the conducted lectures, I thought of applying the tips I learned from the session, especially when writing to the lecturers at my university. I also created a professional signature and started using it when composing emails.
